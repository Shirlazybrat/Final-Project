Founders

Shirlette Chambers

After being a Bridesmaid in more than five weddings and dealing with avoidable issues, Shirlette decided to make the lives of brides and their bridal party much easier by creating an application that will allow for collaborative planning of the wedding, bridal shower and more, all in one central location that all members will have access to. She strongly believes that the one of the happiest days of your life should be the most enjoyed and stress free.

Tammara Chambers

As a bride-to-be, Tammara needed a way to do all of her planning and keep in contact with her bridal party in a convenient way that would allow for everyone to stay on the same page. Planning a wedding is stressfull enough and not everyone can afford to have an in-person planner to help them along the way. With this application, brides can keep all the details in one place and the bridal party will not miss a beat.
